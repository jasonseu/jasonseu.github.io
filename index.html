About me

I am currently a forth-year Ph.D. candidate in CPSS Lab, Southeast University. I am supervised by Prof. Jiuxin Cao. I received bachelor's degree in Compute Science in Hohai University (HHU) in 2016 and master's degree in Computer Science in Southeast University (SEU) in 2019. My current research interests are deep learning, language and vision and social network.
